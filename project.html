Why Think Global?

Think Global is a fictitious, not-for-profit organization in New Zealand that offers international exchange programs. Think Global gives New Zealand high school students the ablilty to test the waters and find out what the world is holding for them. with this amazing opportunity, with the help of the Think Global team, you can have the chance to make a difference. With our build a home, we aim to build over 100 schools and villages around the world. Would you like to make a difference?
